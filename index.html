Front-end programmer with a background both in Informatics as well as Comparative Literature.

I started my time at NTNU with a year of comparative literature-classes. Being kind of a book-nerd all my childhood, this seemed like a natural place to begin my educational journey. I didn't take long before I understood that I wanted to continue, and five years later I had completed my master's degree. During the study I quickly discovered that web developing was what I loved the most. I hope to find a job where I can continue to develop my front-end skills, but also get to use my creative abilities.
